Containers Overview

There are 3 linked "Containers" in Pathways.  They are under the Case menu: 

Registration

Pre Op

Case History

Containers allow navigation through the various patient information forms without the need to go through multiple independent menus.  

All Container forms have a Tree View on the left for case selection.  Simply open a date by clicking on the  or double-click the date to see the available cases.  Cancelled visits for which the visit information was retained can be included by checking the Canceled Visits? check box. These will display in bright blue.

Once a patient has been selected, all additional forms and tabs within that container can be completed for the same patient simply by selecting the next tab on a form or the next form on the navigation ribbon.

Look for all of the icons seen below to move between forms within the Container.
