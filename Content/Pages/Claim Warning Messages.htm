Claim Warning Messages

Operation > Create Claims

HST scrubs claims prior to generation to maximize the submission of clean claims. Any errors that are identified when the claims are scrubbed will appear in the Information column of the Claim Review grid, and the Generate check box will not be checked for these claims.

  • To correct any errors identified, select the claim and right click in the Information column cell. Select the form in which the error exists and that form will be invoked to make and save the required corrections.
  • Select to ensure that all errors and/or omissions have been corrected prior to claim generation.
