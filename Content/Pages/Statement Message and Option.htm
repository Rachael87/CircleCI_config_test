Statement Message and Option

Prior to creating statements you need to create your statement messages and associate them with actions and inactivity.  You may want to ask yourself, "How do I make my statements efficient and limit my calls?" to complete this task.

You will use 2 forms: Statement Message and Statement Option.  It may be helpful to have the both forms open at the same time.

Best Practice

Statement messages should ideally be crafted to tell the patient exactly why they received the statement.  You may choose, for instance, to have a message such as “Thank you for your payment; any remaining balance is your responsibility” attached to your Patient Payment trigger; this will ensure that both you and the patient understand exactly why the statement was printed at this time.

 

NOTE:  HST includes a built-in feature that produces a statement once per month. There may be circumstances in which a patient does not hit an inactivity trigger within 30 days of the last statement; in this case an automatic statement would be produced.  The "monthly" reason will clearly display on the statement reason when you generate the form and within the Account statement records.

 

Statement Messages

Administration > Configuration > Statement Message

Click New to enter a statement message.  After entering the Message Description and Message Body, click Save.  

The message is now available in the Statement Option form.


Statement Option

Administration > Configuration > Statement Option

 

Use the Statement Option form to associate the messages you created in Statement Message with the appropriate Trigger Options and Inactivity Messages, and set additional configuration.

  • If you want to suppress statements in the event of a credit balance, check "Suppress Statement On" Credit Balance
  • Select the "Special Criteria":
  • Show Balance Only: no individual transactions will appear on a statement; just a ‘Balance Due’.
  • Show New Tx Only: Transactions will only appear on a statement one time; old transactions on subsequent statements will be rolled up into a ‘Balance Forward” line.
  • Show All Tx: all transactions from non-suppressed visits will appear on every statement.
  • Monthly:  This box will be checked by HST staff if you have elected to use only 30-day inactivity increments and want a statement only monthly.  This feature ensures that a patient will get a statement once per month, and only once per month.
  • Min Days Between Statements:  is set to 15 days by default.  This prevents patients from receiving back-to-back statements in the few scenarios wherein they might otherwise be produced.
  • Statements Per Batch:  If you want to limit the number of statements that will be put into a single batch, specify the maximum here; if the number of statements produced exceeds that number, Pathways will create multiple batches to accommodate the total number created.
