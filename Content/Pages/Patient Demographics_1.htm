Case > Registration > Demographics

 

 Patient Demographics: Basic Information tab

The Basic Information tab on the Demographic form is where any demographic information that was not entered in the appointment will be completed. The Additional ID 1 field is often used for account numbers from a previous database for centers that have transferred data from another system. This provides a cross reference to any accounts remaining in the legacy database.

On this tab, there are also check boxes to:

Exclude From Survey? - This box can be checked for patients that do not want to be included in patient satisfaction surveys or patients with repeat visits (such as pain management) who do not need to receive a survey for every visit.

Exclude From Portal? - This box will only be visible for centers that are utilizing the HST Patient Portal. It can be checked for patients who do not want to receive text messages and/or e-mails regarding signing up for and managing their records via the portal.

E-mail None? - This box can be checked for patients who do not have or choose not to provide an e-mail address.

Comments entered on the Patient Demographics Basic Information tab are for informational purposes only and will not print on schedules or reports.

Scheduling Alerts entered on this tab will generate a hyperlink on the current appointment that can be selected to view the alert and will also appear as a pop-up alert if patient is scheduled in the future.

The Total Disability Date can be recorded for disabled patients as well as any Permanent Diagnosis that may be relevant to patient care, such as diabetes. If there is more than one permanent diagnosis, the Comments field can be utilized to record additional information.

 

NOTE: Though Race, Religion, Language, and Ethnicity can also be entered on this tab, information entered here will not be included in state reporting. These fields are for center statistics and/or studies only.

 

 Best Practice

Enter the most-frequently used ZIP Codes in Code Table Maintenance. When entering a patient address, simply enter the ZIP Code in the patient address area and hit Tab. The city and state will auto-fill.

 Patient Demographics: HIPAA tab

This tab can be utilized to document requests for and release of patient records.

Clicking the  button displays the records for viewing or data entry.  Any entries / changes must be Saved before the  button will be available.
