Scheduling FAQ

 

Q: How do I cancel an appointment/visit?

A: There are 2 places that a user may go to cancel a visit. The Appointment screen and Patient Visit  forms.

On each of these forms there is a drop-down field for Cancel Reason. A reason must be entered before a visit can be canceled. The center may add to this list by editing the Cancel Reason Table in Code Table Maintenance.

Once a reason is selected, click Cancel Appointment, and upon confirmation that visit will be removed. A list of canceled visits is available on the SC1001 - Cancelled Appointments report.

NOTE:: If transactions have been posted to a visit, those transactions must be reversed and/or payments refunded and the visit returned to zero balance before the visit may be cancelled. (See Billing Transactions FAQ.)

 

Q: How do I move a Visit?

A: There are 4 ways to move a visit on the Appointment Sheet:

  • Cut and Paste: On the Appointment Sheet, click once on the visit to be rescheduled to highlight it, then right click and choose ‘Cut’. Then, click once on the time slot to move the appointment to new Time, Day, Room etc.. Right click in the desired time slot and choose ‘Paste’.
    • Pathways will not allow another case to be scheduled until the ‘Cut’ Appointment is handled. Choose ‘Undo Cut’ from the right click menu to return a ‘Cut’ appointment to its previous location.
  • Edit Date/Time: Open the appointment by double clicking on it. Change the Room/Date/Time as desired and save the changes.
    • Unless certain that time slot is available, blindly changing the Room/Date/Time of an appointment may result in double booking. Be sure to review available time slots to avoid this.
  • Drag and Drop: On the Appointment Sheet, click once on the visit to be rescheduled to highlight it, then hover the mouse over the border of the visit. When the cursor changes, left click and hold to 'drag' appointment to new time slot. Release mouse to 'drop' in the new time slot.
  • Swap Room: Use the swap room feature on the Appointment Sheet: Utility tab to move one or many visits from one room to another.

 

Q: How do I remove a room from the Appointment Sheet?

A: On the Appointment Sheet,click  in the bottom left corner to open the Appointment Sheet Configuration. Uncheck the Visible check box for any rooms to be hidden then Save.

NOTE: The Appointment Sheet must be closed and re-opened in order to see configuration changes.

 

Q: I'm getting the error "No Qty defined for Equipment" when scheduling a case.  How do I fix it?

A: Pull up the equipment record in Inventory > Item Master.  

Make sure that the necesary boxes are checked in the Equipment section:

Click  to open the Equipment form.  Click New, enter the correlating Item Code from the Item Master.  Click Save.

Each piece of equipment will need to be added as a new record. . To enter 3 C-Arms for example, 1) Click New 2) enter the Item Code and 3) Save, 3 times.  

To update or see existing equipment for an Item Code, click on the arrows or Move next on the bottom right hand side of the Sub Form Toolbar.
